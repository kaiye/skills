# Whiteboard Edit Prompt — v7 (HANDWRITING LOCKED)

Use this as an **image-edit** prompt (photo → online clean diagram) with a stronger constraint on **handwritten text**.

```text
Digitize this photographed whiteboard into a clean ONLINE diagram while keeping a strong HAND-DRAWN whiteboard feel.

PRESERVE SEMANTICS + LAYOUT: Keep the exact same structure, grouping, arrows, numbering, and content placement as the original. Do NOT add or remove ideas.

CRITICAL TEXT STYLE (must be stable):
- All Chinese and English text MUST look like natural handwritten marker text.
- Absolutely NO typeset fonts, NO computer-printed typography, NO perfect kerning.
- Keep the original wording exactly; do not paraphrase.

HAND-DRAWN LOOK:
- Blue marker strokes with slight wobble and varying thickness like a real whiteboard marker.
- Imperfect but neat: lines not perfectly straight, corners slightly rounded.

CLEANUP:
- Straighten perspective.
- Remove glare and shadows.
- Pure white background.
- Increase contrast for readability.

COLORS:
- Lines and arrows: blue marker ink (#1E5EFF).
- Text: dark gray/black (#222).
- Minimal extra colors.

OUTPUT: 4:3 aspect ratio, 4K, looks like a digitally redrawn sketchnote whiteboard export.
```
